2 Bedrooms Apartment

Spacious 2 bedroom apartment with a Queen sized bed, 2 double sofa beds and a kitchenette. The room is perfectly suitable either for a big family or a couple, or business tourists who enjoy comfortable stay. However, the room is perfectly suitable for a family up to 6 people. Room sleeps: up to 6 guests. The only one room located on the 1st floor.

Laconically designed interior is complimented with quality wooden furniture that fits into the ambience of a building.
